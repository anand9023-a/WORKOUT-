Aesthetic Mass Plan

Target: 53kg to 65kg+ (Greek God Physique)

Rules for Success (Must Follow)
  • The 4-Second Tempo: Niche jaate waqt 4 second ginein. Slow movements se muscle fibre break hote hain.
  • Progressive Overload: Jab 12 reps aaram se hone lagen, toh bag (backpack) mein eenth (bricks) bhar kar pehniye.
  • Rest Days: Wed, Sat, aur Sun ko bilkul workout nahi karna. Body growth rest mein hoti hai.
  • Failure: Har set mein tab tak rep karein jab tak sharir doosra rep na kar paaye.
